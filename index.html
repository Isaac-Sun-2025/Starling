Meet RoboSchoolies

A team of innovative minds pushing the boundaries of robotics in the FIRST Robotics Competition since 2018.

Our Mission

RoboSchoolies is dedicated to inspiring students to pursue careers in STEM through hands-on experience in robotics engineering, programming, and business management. We believe in fostering innovation, teamwork, and leadership skills that extend beyond the competition floor.

Our Vision

We aim to create a sustainable program that provides equal opportunities for all students to engage with cutting-edge technology, develop professional skills, and build connections with industry leaders.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to regional champions, follow our path of growth and innovation.

2018

Team Founded

Started with just 12 students and 2 mentors, participating in our first regional competition.

2020

First Major Award

Won the Rookie All-Star Award at the Pacific Northwest Regional, qualifying for the World Championship.

2022

Team Expansion

Grew to 25 members and secured our first major corporate sponsorship, allowing us to build a more advanced robot.

2024

Regional Champions

Won the Pacific Northwest Regional Championship and the Excellence in Engineering Award, showcasing our team's growth and technical expertise.
